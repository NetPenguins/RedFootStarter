Our Mission

Apex Predator Alert was created to assist in the reporting and identification of wildlife that can pose a threat to life and property. We aim to educate the public on the dangers of certain predators in the wild and establish a healthy understanding as to why such wildlife might attack. The most important thing to remember is that we have encroached on their land torn down their homes and driven them out of their habitats in which they have grown and evolved over thousand/millions of years. This devastating action by humanity has been further exasperated as we continue to develop our communities in the ashes of what was rightfully theirs. When we find a mountain lion in our back yard it is not because they want to hurt you or your pets, it is because we have built our homes on top of their previous home. We have put our resources over theirs and they are simply trying to survive. When they feel threatened, they will defend themselves the same way we defend ourselves. Please do not fear the wildlife you have built your life around but rather embrace the opportunity to live alongside them providing them the opportunity to grow and prosper in a world that is unlike their previously known world. Use this system to establish a social network of understanding, if your neighbor reports they saw a bear in the area simply make the accommodation to be more cautious about your animals and yourself routines rather than hunt down an innocent animal simply trying to live on the land we have taken from them.
